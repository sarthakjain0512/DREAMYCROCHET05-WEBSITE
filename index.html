Made with Devotion

Stitch by Stitch Journey

Witness how raw yarn becomes a memory-holding art piece

1. Yarn Selection

We source the softest, premium organic cotton yarns. Free from chemicals, durable, and featuring rich pastel dyes.

2. Hand Crocheting

Using fine needle points, our crafters stitch each petal carefully. It takes hours of focus to form consistent stitches.

3. Details & Assembly

We wire the stems for flexible structural balance, matching petals together to build complete, lifelike blossoms.

4. Elegant Wrap

Finally, we hand-wrap the collection in classic craft papers, tieing silk ribbons and attaching handwritten notes of joy.
